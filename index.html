About The Project

Hip-Hop Legend was made just for fun as an experiment of mine. Recently, I watched a documentary series called Hip-Hop Evolution, which tells the story behind the Hip-Hop's creation on the South Bronx - New York City (as well as The Get Down). And by seeing the interviews with the firsts MCs and DJs of the world, I got spirited about that.

So, I watched a tutorial video by Wes Bos that uses the audio API from HTML5 with vanilla JavaScript. I took advantage of that and made this side-project in a day and half, using beats and loops from Hip-Hop instead of a drum kit like in the tutorial. It was an opportunity to improve a little bit of my knowledge and build something without jQuery for the first time in my life (although the code is not the very best).

Thank you for your attention, and feel free to use the code or send me a message if you want.
